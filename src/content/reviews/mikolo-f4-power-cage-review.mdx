## The LAT Pulldown Test

This is where the Mikolo F4 earns its price. The dual-track pulley system is smooth and handles weight cleanly. We loaded 200 lbs on the weight pin and did lat pulldowns, cable rows, tricep pushdowns, and bicep curls. Zero cable friction, zero jerkiness.

For comparison, adding a LAT attachment to a budget rack like the Fitness Reality 810XLT costs another $200-300. The Mikolo F4 includes it from the start.

## Who Should Buy It?

**Buy the Mikolo F4 if:**
- You want an all-in-one home gym solution
- You value cable work (lat pulldowns, rows, tricep pushdowns)
- You're on a budget but want advanced features
- You have the space for a larger cage

**Skip it if:**
- You only need basic power rack functionality
- Space is very tight
- You already have cable equipment
