## Box Jump Safety

Plyo boxes are not without risk. Wooden boxes are forgiving on shins (steel will shred your skin), but you still need to follow basic safety:

1. **Start at the lowest height** — 20" is enough for most beginners
2. **Land softly** — quiet landings = good landings
3. **Step down, don't jump down** — landing impact ruins knees over time
4. **Check for splinters** — sand any rough edges before first use
5. **Place on stable flooring** — never on carpet or uneven surface
