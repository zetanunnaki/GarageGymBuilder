## Yes4All vs Bumper Plates

This is the key decision for anyone adding plates:

**Yes4All Cast Iron** ($1/lb):
- Cheaper per pound
- Thinner (more fit on the bar)
- LOUD when dropped
- Can crack concrete if dropped

**Rubber Bumper Plates** ($1.50-2/lb):
- More expensive
- Thicker (fewer fit on bar)
- Safe to drop
- Required for Olympic lifts

**Our recommendation:** Get cast iron for squats/bench/overhead press. Get bumper plates for deadlifts and Olympic lifts if you need to drop them.
