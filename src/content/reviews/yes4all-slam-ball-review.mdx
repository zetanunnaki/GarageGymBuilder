## Slam Ball vs Medicine Ball: Important Difference

These get confused all the time. They're not the same:

- **Medicine ball:** Bouncy, soft outer shell, 4-15 lbs. Made for tossing and catching.
- **Slam ball:** No bounce, dense PVC outer, 10-50 lbs. Made for slamming into the floor.

If you bounce a slam ball, you'll get a black eye when it doesn't bounce back. If you slam a medicine ball, you'll destroy it. Buy the right tool for your goal.

## What Weight to Buy

Most people want one slam ball, not a set. Pick the right weight:

**Beginners:**
- Women: 10-15 lbs
- Men: 15-20 lbs

**Intermediate:**
- Women: 15-25 lbs
- Men: 20-30 lbs

**Advanced:**
- Women: 25-40 lbs
- Men: 30-50 lbs

A 20-30 lb slam ball is the universal sweet spot — heavy enough for power training, light enough for high-rep conditioning.

## Best Slam Ball Exercises

1. **Standard Slam** — overhead to floor, build power
2. **Side Slam** — rotation power, oblique training
3. **Thruster Slam** — squat to overhead slam, full body
4. **Wall Ball** — toss to wall target, conditioning
5. **Clean to Slam** — Olympic style + slam combo
6. **Carry + Slam** — farmer's walk + slam, brutal cardio

10 minutes of slam ball work equals 30 minutes of moderate cardio.
