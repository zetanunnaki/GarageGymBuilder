Unlike most racks sold as "add-ons only," the Sportsroyals comes loaded with attachments:

- Main power cage structure
- Dual adjustable cable crossover system
- LAT pulldown bar

- Tricep rope
- Landmine attachment

This is genuinely a complete home gym in one box.

## Assembly Reality

Let's be honest — assembly is a project. It took us 5 hours with two people to fully assemble the rack and all the cable systems. The instructions have diagrams but some steps require inference.

**Pro tip:** Don't start assembly late at night. Set aside a full Saturday morning.

## Who Is This For?

The Sportsroyals Power Cage is the home gym equivalent of buying a commercial gym multi-station — except for a fraction of the price. It's ideal for lifters who want variety in one piece of equipment.

### Best For
- Home gym builders with 8x8 ft of space
- Lifters who want cable work without a separate machine
- Beginners building their first complete home gym
- Families sharing a home gym

### Not For
- Minimalist setups
- Powerlifting-only training (simpler rack is fine)
- Small apartments or tight spaces
- Serious competition lifters (get a simpler premium rack)
