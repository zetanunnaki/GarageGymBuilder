## Setup and Anchoring

The TRX needs a sturdy overhead anchor:

- **Outdoor:** Tree branches, pull-up bars, swing sets
- **Indoor:** Power rack pull-up bar, ceiling joist hook, door anchor (included)
- **Travel:** Door anchor in any inward-opening door

The included door anchor is the most common setup for travelers. It loops over the top of an inward-opening door, then closes against the door for a secure anchor.

## Best TRX Exercises

The TRX excels at bodyweight strength training. Best exercises:

1. **TRX Row** — back and biceps
2. **TRX Push-Up** — chest with instability
3. **TRX Pistol Squat** — single-leg strength
4. **TRX Plank** — core stability
5. **TRX Hamstring Curl** — posterior chain
6. **TRX Atomic Push-Up** — chest + core combo

Adjust difficulty by changing your body angle — closer to vertical = easier, closer to horizontal = harder.
