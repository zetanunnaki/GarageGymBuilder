## How Much Weight Should You Use?

Start lighter than you think:

**Beginner:**
- Walking: 10-15 lbs
- Push-ups: 12 lbs
- Pull-ups: 10 lbs

**Intermediate:**
- Walking: 20-25 lbs
- Push-ups: 20-25 lbs
- Pull-ups: 15-25 lbs

**Advanced:**
- Walking: 35-40 lbs
- Push-ups: 30-40 lbs
- Pull-ups: 30+ lbs (you may need a 60+ lb vest)

The 40 lb max RUNFast covers beginner through intermediate users. Elite athletes need a heavier vest.

## Best Weight Vest Workouts

### The "Murph" Workout (Memorial Day Tradition)
- 1 mile run
- 100 pull-ups
- 200 push-ups
- 300 air squats
- 1 mile run
- All wearing a 20 lb vest

### Weighted Walking (Rucking)
30-60 minutes of walking with a 20-40 lb vest. Burns 2-3x more calories than unweighted walking. Easy on joints. The cheat code of cardio.

### Vest Push-Up Pyramid
1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 4, 3, 2, 1 push-ups with rest as needed. Wear a 20-30 lb vest. Total: 25 push-ups in ~5 minutes that feel like 100.
