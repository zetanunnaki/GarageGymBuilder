## Why Fixed Dumbbells Beat Adjustables

For some users, fixed cast iron is actually better than adjustable:

- **Faster between exercises** — no dial adjustment, just grab and go
- **Indestructible** — drop them all day, nothing breaks
- **Cheaper per pound** — about $1 per pound vs $5+ for adjustables
- **Better balance** — solid cast iron feels more natural than mechanical sleeves
- **No maintenance** — no moving parts to break

The trade-off: storage. A full set takes up significant space.

## Who Should Buy Them

**Buy fixed hex dumbbells if:**
- You have garage space for a dumbbell rack
- You want indestructible equipment
- You hate adjusting things mid-workout
- You drop dumbbells (CrossFit, HIIT)
- You want the cheapest per-pound option

**Buy adjustable dumbbells instead if:**
- You have limited storage space
- You want all weights in one unit
- You'll never drop them
- You prefer modern equipment
