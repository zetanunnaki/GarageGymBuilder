## Who Should Buy This?

The R-3 is for lifters who:
- Want a rack they'll never outgrow or replace
- Value American manufacturing and quality
- Plan to invest in attachments over time
- Are willing to pay a premium for the best

If you're on a tight budget or unsure about long-term commitment to home gym training, start with a Titan T-3 or REP PR-4000 and upgrade later.
