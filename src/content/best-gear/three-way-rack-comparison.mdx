The three most popular budget power racks on Amazon are the **Fitness Reality 810XLT** ($330), the **Mikolo F4 2.0** ($489), and the **Sportsroyals Power Cage** ($549). All three have 4.5+ star ratings with thousands of reviews. All three are under $600. But they serve very different lifters.

After testing all three for months, here's our definitive three-way comparison.

## The Quick Answer

**Fitness Reality 810XLT ($330):** Best for beginners. Cheapest functional rack, simple design, proven with 5,000+ reviews. No cable system.

**Mikolo F4 2.0 ($489):** Best all-around value. Includes LAT pulldown + low row cable. 1,200 lb capacity. The sweet-spot purchase for most home gym builders.

**Sportsroyals Power Cage ($549):** Best features. Full cable crossover system, 1,600 lb capacity, most attachments included. The most complete home gym in one box.

## Build Quality

### Fitness Reality 810XLT
- **Steel:** 2x2" 14-gauge
- **Weight capacity:** 800 lbs
- **Feel:** Light but solid for budget price
- **Notes:** Slight flex under heavy squats (normal for 14-gauge)

### Mikolo F4 2.0
- **Steel:** 2x2" 12-gauge (thicker)
- **Weight capacity:** 1,200 lbs
- **Feel:** Noticeably heavier and more solid than the Fitness Reality
- **Notes:** Zero flex under heavy loads

### Sportsroyals Power Cage
- **Steel:** 2x2" heavy-duty
- **Weight capacity:** 1,600 lbs
- **Feel:** The heaviest of the three, most stable
- **Notes:** Cable crossover adds significant structural complexity

**Winner:** Sportsroyals (highest capacity), then Mikolo F4, then Fitness Reality.

## Who Should Buy Which?

### Buy the Fitness Reality 810XLT if:
- You're a beginner on a strict budget
- You don't need cable work (LAT pulldown, rows)
- You want the cheapest functional rack
- You want the most reviews/proven track record
- You want the simplest, fastest assembly

### Buy the Mikolo F4 2.0 if:
- You want a power rack + LAT pulldown in one purchase
- You value cable work without buying separate equipment
- You want higher weight capacity than the Fitness Reality
- You're willing to spend 50% more for significant features
- This is the **best choice for 60% of home gym builders**

### Buy the Sportsroyals Power Cage if:
- You want a complete home gym in one box
- You want cable crossover (chest flyes, cable curls)
- You have 8x8 ft of dedicated space
- You want maximum features per dollar
- You don't mind a longer assembly

## Our Final Pick

For **most home gym builders**, we recommend the **Mikolo F4 2.0**. It hits the sweet spot of price, features, and capacity. The LAT pulldown alone adds $300 worth of functionality compared to simpler racks.

That said:
- **Pure beginners on a budget:** Fitness Reality 810XLT
- **Feature-maximalists:** Sportsroyals Power Cage
- **Sweet spot value:** Mikolo F4 2.0

All three are excellent choices that will last years of home gym use.
