## Which Cardio Machine Should You Buy?

**For maximum calorie burn:** Assault AirBike or Concept2 RowErg. Both are full-body and scale infinitely.

**For quiet home training:** Sunny Spin Bike or Sunny Rower. Magnetic resistance is nearly silent.

**For small spaces:** XTERRA Treadmill or Sunny Rower — both fold for storage.

**For budget under $300:** Sunny Rower ($250) or XTERRA Treadmill ($250). Both are excellent value.

**For a lifter who hates cardio:** Assault AirBike. It makes cardio so brutal that it's over in 10-15 minutes. Efficient suffering.
