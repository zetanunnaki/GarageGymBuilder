## The Buying Order

If you're starting from scratch, buy specialty bars in this order:

1. **EZ Curl Bar** ($55) — adds arm isolation your straight bar can't do comfortably
2. **Landmine Attachment** ($20) — unlocks 10+ exercises for the price of a pizza
3. **Trap Bar** ($200) — transforms deadlift day and adds farmer walks
4. **Safety Squat Bar** ($180) — the luxury pick for lifters with shoulder issues

Total: **$455** for all four. But the first two ($75 total) cover 80% of what most lifters need.

## Do You Really Need Specialty Bars?

Honestly? No. A straight Olympic barbell does everything. Specialty bars make specific exercises *more comfortable* and *safer*, but they're not required.

The exception: if you have a shoulder injury that limits back squatting or a wrist injury that makes curling painful, the safety squat bar and EZ curl bar aren't luxury items — they're the difference between training and not training.
