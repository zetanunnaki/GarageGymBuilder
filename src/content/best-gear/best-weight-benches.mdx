## What Matters in a Weight Bench

### Pad Width
Competition benches are 12" wide. Budget benches are often 10-10.5". If you're a bigger lifter or bench heavy, the extra width matters for shoulder blade retraction.

### Weight Capacity
Don't just add your body weight plus the bar. Factor in the dynamic force of lifting. A 700 lb rated bench gives most people plenty of headroom.

### Adjustability
- **Flat only** — cheapest, most stable, limited exercises
- **Adjustable (flat to 90°)** — most versatile, slight stability trade-off
- **FID (flat/incline/decline)** — maximum versatility, often more expensive

### Stability
The bench should not wobble, rock, or slide during heavy pressing. This is where cheap benches fail most often. Test with heavy dumbbell presses — that's where instability shows up.
