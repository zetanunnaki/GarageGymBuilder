## What to Look For in Adjustable Dumbbells

### Weight Range
Most people need at least 5-50 lbs per dumbbell. If you're already benching 80+ lb dumbbells, look at loadable systems.

### Change Mechanism
- **Dial systems** (Bowflex) — fastest changes, best for supersets
- **Pin-based** (PowerBlock) — more durable, stackable
- **Spin-lock** — cheapest, slowest to adjust

### Durability
The number one killer of adjustable dumbbells is dropping them. If you do any movement where a drop is possible, invest in the more durable option.

## The Bottom Line

For most home gym owners, the **Bowflex SelectTech 552** hits the sweet spot of price, weight range, and convenience. Just don't drop them.
