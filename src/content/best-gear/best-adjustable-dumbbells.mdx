## What to Look For in Adjustable Dumbbells

### Weight Range
Most people need at least 5-50 lbs per dumbbell. If you're already benching 80+ lb dumbbells, look at loadable systems or the PowerBlock 90.

### Change Mechanism
- **Dial systems** (Bowflex) — fastest changes, best for supersets
- **Pin-based** (PowerBlock) — more durable, drop-safe
- **Spin-lock** — cheapest, slowest to adjust

### Durability
The number one killer of adjustable dumbbells is dropping them. If you do any movement where a drop is possible, invest in the PowerBlock. The Bowflex's fragile dial mechanism won't survive a drop from lifting height.
