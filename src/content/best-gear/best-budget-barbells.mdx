## What Makes a Good Budget Barbell?

### Tensile Strength
Look for bars rated at 150K+ PSI. Anything below that will develop a permanent bend under heavy squats.

### Knurling
Aggressive enough to hold without gloves, but not so sharp it tears your hands during high-rep deadlifts. Most budget bars nail this.

### Sleeve Rotation
Olympic lifts need spinning sleeves. If you only squat/bench/dead, bushing sleeves are fine and much cheaper than bearings.

### Whip
Budget bars are typically stiff (no whip), which is actually better for squats and bench. If you're doing Olympic lifts, save up for a dedicated Oly bar.
