## How We Test Power Racks

Every rack in our testing goes through a standardized evaluation:

1. **Stability Testing** - Loaded squats at 80%+ capacity to check for wobble
2. **J-Cup Quality** - Barbell contact surface and ease of racking
3. **Assembly Time** - Timed from box opening to first rep
