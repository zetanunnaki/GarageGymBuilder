The trap bar (hex bar) might be the single best specialty barbell you can add to a home gym. It turns the deadlift into one of the safest, most joint-friendly exercises in existence — and it's arguably better than a straight bar for most non-competitive lifters.

## Why a Trap Bar?

### Deadlift Advantages
- **Less lower back stress** — neutral grip and centered load reduce shear force on the spine
- **More quad involvement** — more upright torso position recruits quads
- **Easier to learn** — beginners pick up the trap bar deadlift in minutes
- **Higher weight potential** — most people can trap bar deadlift 10-20% more than conventional

### Beyond Deadlifts
- **Farmer's walks** — best grip and core exercise available
- **Shrugs** — neutral grip is more comfortable than straight bar
- **Overhead press** — some trap bars allow neutral-grip pressing
- **Jump squats** — explosive training with lower injury risk
- **Rows** — bent-over rows with neutral grip

## Open vs Closed Trap Bar

### Closed (Traditional Hex Bar)
- Fully enclosed hexagonal frame
- You step into the center
- Cannot do farmer's walks (bar hits your legs)
- Generally cheaper

### Open (Rackable Trap Bar)
- Open back design — you can walk forward and backward
- Enables farmer's walks and carries
- Often fits in a rack for elevated starts
- More expensive but more versatile

**Our recommendation:** Open-back trap bar. The ability to do farmer's walks alone justifies the extra cost.

## What to Look For

### Weight Capacity
Most trap bars handle 500-700 lbs. If you're deadlifting 500+, look for bars rated at 750+ lbs.

### Handle Height
Many trap bars have dual handles — high and low.
- **High handles:** Reduced range of motion, easier on the back, great for beginners
- **Low handles:** Full range of motion, standard deadlift depth

### Sleeve Length
Longer sleeves = more plates. Look for at least 10" of loadable sleeve length on each side.

### Bar Weight
Trap bars are heavier than straight bars. Typical weight: 55-75 lbs (vs 45 lbs for a standard bar).
