This is the most common question we get. Here's the honest comparison:

| Battery | 150 min | 150 min |
| Noise Level | Moderate | Very quiet |
| Stall Force | 20 lbs | 15 lbs |
| Attachments | 1 included | 2 included |

**Buy the Theragun Mini if:** You want more percussive power for deep knots and dense muscle tissue (glutes, IT band).

**Buy the Hypervolt GO 2 if:** Noise matters (early mornings, shared spaces, office) and you prefer vibration therapy over hard percussion.

| Timing | Tool | Duration |
