## The Power Rack: Mikolo F4 2.0

<ProductCard productId="mikolo-f4-power-cage" badge="Premium Build Pick" />

At this budget, the Mikolo F4 is the right choice. You get a proper power rack PLUS a built-in LAT pulldown and low row cable system, saving you $300-500 over buying a cable system separately. Read our [Mikolo F4 review](/reviews/mikolo-f4-power-cage-review/) or the [Mikolo vs Fitness Reality comparison](/best-gear/mikolo-f4-vs-fitness-reality-810xlt/).

## The Barbell: Synergee Games + CAP Plates

<ProductCard productId="synergee-olympic-barbell" badge="Upgraded Bar" />

Skip the entry-level CAP bar that comes with the 300 lb set. Upgrade to the **Synergee Games Barbell** ($200) for needle bearings and 190K PSI tensile strength. But still buy the CAP set for the plates — 255 lbs of cast iron for $340 is unbeatable.

**Net result:** Two barbells (Synergee + CAP bar) + 255 lbs of plates for $540 total.

Then add another 180 lbs with Yes4All 45 lb pairs for $180, bringing your total plate weight to 435 lbs.

Read our [CAP vs Synergee comparison](/best-gear/cap-vs-synergee-barbell/) and [Yes4All Olympic Plates review](/reviews/yes4all-olympic-plates-review/).
