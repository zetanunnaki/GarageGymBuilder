## The Rack: Rogue Monster Lite R-3

At $5K, you can afford the best. The R-3's 3x3 11-gauge steel, lifetime warranty, and massive attachment ecosystem make it the centerpiece of any serious gym. You'll never need to replace it.

## The Barbell: Rogue Ohio Bar

The Rogue Ohio Bar is the most popular all-purpose barbell in the world. 190K PSI tensile strength, 28.5mm shaft, center knurling, and available in multiple finishes.

- **Why not the bar in the CAP set?** At $5K, you deserve a bar that matches the rack. The Ohio Bar will handle anything you throw at it for decades.

## The Plates: Bumper Plates

At this budget, skip cast iron. Bumper plates are safer for deadlifts, Olympic lifts, and training in a garage where dropped weights could damage the floor.

**Our recommended set:**
- 4x 45 lb bumpers
- 2x 25 lb bumpers
- 2x 10 lb bumpers
- 2x 5 lb change plates
- 2x 2.5 lb change plates

Total: 370 lbs — enough for advanced training with room to add more.

## The Bench: REP AB-5200

The AB-5200 is the gold standard adjustable bench. 1,000 lb capacity, 12" wide pad, zero-gap design, and multiple positions from decline to 90 degrees. This is the bench you see in every serious home gym.

## The Cardio: Rogue Echo Bike

<ProductCard productId="rogue-echo-bike" badge="Conditioning King" />

The Echo Bike handles HIIT, steady-state, warm-ups, and finishers. It's built like a tank and will outlive everything else in your gym.

## The Dumbbells: Bowflex SelectTech 552

<ProductCard productId="bowflex-selecttech-552" badge="Space Saver" />

Adjustable dumbbells save hundreds of square feet compared to a fixed dumbbell rack. The SelectTech 552s cover 5-52.5 lbs — enough for all dumbbell accessory work.
