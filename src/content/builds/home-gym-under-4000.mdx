## The Cardio: Concept2 RowErg

<ProductCard productId="concept2-rowerg" badge="Gold Standard" />

The Concept2 RowErg is the only rower that belongs in a premium home gym. 40+ years of proven durability, Olympic-grade quality, and the industry-standard PM5 monitor. Read our [Concept2 RowErg review](/reviews/concept2-rowerg-review/).

**Why Concept2 over an air bike at this level?** The rower is more versatile for longer sessions and low-impact cardio. Serious home gyms often have both, but if you can only have one at $4K, the rower is more useful.

## What You're NOT Getting at $4K

To hit the $4K budget, we skipped:

- **Premium bench** (REP AB-5200 at $549) — the FLYBIRD works, but you could upgrade
- **Secondary cardio** (Rogue Echo Bike at $945) — the RowErg covers most needs
- **Specialty bars** (SSB, trap bar) — add as needed
- **Full bumper plate set** — cast iron is fine for most uses
- **Commercial-grade flooring** — horse stall mats are 80% as good

## Upgrade Path to $5K

If you can stretch to $5,000, add:

1. **REP AB-5200 2.0 Bench** ($549) — replace the FLYBIRD for zero-gap pressing
2. **Specialty barbell** ($200-400) — trap bar or safety squat bar
