Bodybuilding is harder to do at home than powerlifting or CrossFit. It requires cable machines, isolation equipment, and variety — things that home gyms traditionally struggle with. But with the right setup, you can build world-class physiques in your garage.

This guide covers the equipment that actually matters for bodybuilding-style training.

## What Bodybuilding Needs That Other Disciplines Don't

### Variety of Exercises
Powerlifting requires 3-4 lifts. CrossFit requires 10-15 movements. **Bodybuilding benefits from 50+ exercises** across all muscle groups.

### Isolation Work
Cable flyes, lateral raises, cable curls, leg extensions — movements that hit specific muscles in ways compounds can't.

### High Volume Tolerance
Bodybuilders do 15-25+ sets per muscle group per week. Equipment needs to handle high rep counts without wearing down.

### Incremental Loading
Progress is measured in 2.5-5 lb jumps, not 25 lb jumps. You need precise weight adjustments.

## Sample Bodybuilding Workout Split

### Push/Pull/Legs (6 days/week)

**Push Day (Chest/Shoulders/Triceps)**
- Barbell Bench Press: 4x8
- Incline Dumbbell Press: 3x10
- Cable Flyes: 3x12
- Overhead Press: 4x8
- Lateral Raises: 4x15
- Cable Tricep Pushdowns: 3x12
- Skull Crushers: 3x12

**Pull Day (Back/Biceps)**
- Barbell Row: 4x8
- LAT Pulldown: 4x10
- Dumbbell Row: 3x10
- Cable Curls: 4x12
- Face Pulls: 3x15
- Barbell Curls: 3x10
- Hammer Curls: 3x12

**Leg Day**
- Back Squat: 4x8
- Romanian Deadlift: 3x10
- Bulgarian Split Squat: 3x10
- Leg Extension (band): 4x15
- Leg Curl (band): 4x15
- Standing Calf Raise: 4x15

Repeat the cycle twice per week (6 days total, 1 rest day).

## What to Skip for Bodybuilding

- **Air bike** — not useful for bodybuilding, skip unless you also do cardio
- **Rower** — same, unless you want low-impact cardio
- **Kettlebells** — nice-to-have but not essential
- **Trap bar** — not worth the cost for bodybuilding
- **Deadlift jack** — bodybuilders rarely do heavy singles

Focus your budget on cable systems, dumbbells, and variety.
