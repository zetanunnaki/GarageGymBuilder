A barbell and plates is all you need for a complete workout. No rack, no bench, no cable machine. Here are 50 exercises organized by muscle group that you can do with nothing but a loaded bar on the floor.

## Lower Body (15 Exercises)

1. **Conventional Deadlift** — the king of all exercises
2. **Sumo Deadlift** — wider stance, more quad and adductor emphasis
3. **Romanian Deadlift** — hamstring and glute focus
4. **Front Squat** — clean the bar to your shoulders, then squat
5. **Zercher Squat** — bar in the crook of your elbows
6. **Overhead Squat** — snatch grip, bar locked out overhead
7. **Jefferson Squat** — straddle the bar, one foot forward
8. **Barbell Lunge** — forward, reverse, or walking
9. **Barbell Step-Up** — bar on back, step onto a box
10. **Good Morning** — bar on back, hip hinge
11. **Stiff-Leg Deadlift** — straight legs, stretch the hamstrings
12. **Deficit Deadlift** — stand on plates for extended range of motion
13. **Hip Thrust** — back against a bench or box, bar across hips
14. **Calf Raise** — bar on back, raise onto toes
15. **Barbell Hack Squat** — bar behind legs, deadlift with quad emphasis

## Upper Body Push (12 Exercises)

16. **Overhead Press** — strict, standing military press
17. **Push Press** — use leg drive to press overhead
18. **Floor Press** — lie on floor, press the bar from chest
19. **Close-Grip Floor Press** — tricep emphasis
20. **Landmine Press** — wedge bar in corner, press at an angle
21. **Behind-the-Neck Press** — overhead press behind head (if mobility allows)
22. **Z Press** — seated on floor, legs straight, overhead press
23. **Bradford Press** — alternate front and behind-the-neck press
24. **Barbell Push-Up** — hands on bar, wider range of motion
25. **JM Press** — hybrid skull crusher / close-grip press
26. **Pin Press** — press from dead stop position
27. **Svend Press** — squeeze plates together and press outward

## Upper Body Pull (10 Exercises)

28. **Barbell Row** — bent over, pull bar to chest
29. **Pendlay Row** — strict from the floor, reset each rep
30. **Underhand Row** — supinated grip, more bicep involvement
31. **Seal Row** — lie face down on bench, row
32. **Meadows Row** — landmine row, one arm at a time
33. **Upright Row** — pull bar to chin (caution: shoulder impingement risk)
34. **Barbell Curl** — classic bicep builder
35. **Reverse Curl** — overhand grip, forearm emphasis
36. **Barbell Shrug** — traps, hold at top for 2 seconds
37. **Face Pull (landmine)** — rear delt and rotator cuff

## Core & Full Body (13 Exercises)

38. **Clean** — explosive pull from floor to shoulders
39. **Power Clean** — clean caught in quarter squat
40. **Hang Clean** — clean from hang position
41. **Snatch** — floor to overhead in one movement
42. **Clean and Jerk** — full Olympic lift
43. **Barbell Rollout** — ab wheel replacement
44. **Landmine Rotation** — rotational core work
45. **Barbell Carry** — front rack carry, overhead carry, or Zercher carry
46. **Barbell Complex** — string exercises together without putting the bar down
47. **Thruster** — front squat to push press
48. **Man Maker** — push-up, row, clean, press (with barbell variant)
49. **Barbell Sit-Up** — hold bar on chest during sit-ups
50. **Barbell Turkish Get-Up** — advanced full-body stability

## 3 Barbell-Only Workouts

### Workout A: Strength
- Deadlift 5x5
- Overhead Press 5x5
- Barbell Row 5x5
- Barbell Curl 3x10

### Workout B: Conditioning
- 5 rounds: 5 Deadlifts, 5 Cleans, 5 Front Squats, 5 Overhead Press, 5 Rows (no rest between exercises, 90 sec rest between rounds)

### Workout C: Hypertrophy
- Romanian Deadlift 3x12
- Floor Press 3x12
- Pendlay Row 3x10
- Lunges 3x10 each leg
- Barbell Curl 3x12
- Landmine Press 3x12
